## Receive admission enquiries in Gmail

Enquiries are sent **directly to your Gmail** using [Formspree](https://formspree.io).

1. Go to **https://formspree.io** and sign up (free).
2. Click **New form** and set **Email** to: **selvarajan0259@gmail.com**.
3. Copy your **Form ID** (e.g. `xzqkpnwy`).
4. In this project, open **index.html** and find:
   ```text
   action="https://formspree.io/f/REPLACE_WITH_FORMSPREE_ID"
   ```
5. Replace **REPLACE_WITH_FORMSPREE_ID** with your Form ID and save.
6. After that, every “Send Enquiry” will deliver the form data to **selvarajan0259@gmail.com**.
