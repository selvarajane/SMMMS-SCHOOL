Our School

S.Murugaiyan Memorial Model HR.SEC.School (SMMMS) is located at Manalurpet Road, Kilanaikkarai, Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu 606601, India. We offer education from Nursery through Higher Secondary, with a focus on discipline, values, and academic excellence.

Our campus and programmes nurture every child with care and commitment. We celebrate achievement in academics, sports, and events – building confident, responsible citizens.

  • Nursery, Primary & Higher Secondary
  • Manalurpet Road, Kilanaikkarai, Tiruvannamalai
  • Sports, events & cultural activities
