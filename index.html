Our School

S. Murugaiyan Memorial Model Nursery, Primary & Hr.Sec. School (SMMMS) is located at Lurpet Road, Tiruvannamalai. We offer education from Nursery through Higher Secondary, with a focus on discipline, values, and academic excellence.

Our campus and programmes nurture every child with care and commitment. We celebrate achievement in academics, sports, and events — building confident, responsible citizens.

  • Nursery, Primary & Higher Secondary
  • Lurpet Road, Tiruvannamalai
  • Sports, events & cultural activities
